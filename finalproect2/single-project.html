Selena Gomez

Born July 22, 1992, Selena started her journey on "Barney". Soon enough she played Alex Russo on the hit show "Wizards of Waverly Place" on Disney Channel. She pursued both an acting and singing carrer. With hits like "Come and Get It", "Hands to Myself" and "Wolves" she has become the queen of pop. She joined Top Artists in 2016 to promote herself gloabally.

Bea Miller

The 18 year old superstar had her beginnings from the TV show, "The X-Factor". She auditioned when she was 13 years old, and finished in 9th place. Simon Cowell decided to sign her and since then has released two albums. Recently, she joined Top Artists after going on tour with Selena Gomez.

Niall Horan

Also starting his journey on the "X-Factor" Niall was in a boy group called One Direction. Since 2015, the group has been on a hiatus, so he is currently pursuing a solo career. He enjoys writing and playing guitar, providing quality best music for his fans.

Harry Styles

Also a member of One Direction, Harry has a unique-old-school rock type of music. He is also pursuing a solo carrer at the moment. Following officially announcing his break from 1D, he contacted Top Artists to sign a record deal to promote his new album. He has been voted the current Rising Star.

Shawn Mendes

Starting with the app Vine and Youtube, Shawn posted short, 6-second clips of him and singing and playing guitar. Now, he is playing sold-out shows at Madison Square Garden. The native Candian superstar has dominated the world with several of his hits, and already has two albums. After winning the Best Artist Award this year, Shawn has announced that he is currently working on his third album, and is currently on tour promoting his second album, "Illuminate".
